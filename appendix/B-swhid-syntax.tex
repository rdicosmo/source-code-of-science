A core \textsc{swhid} is \swhid{swh:1:<type>:<40-hex>} where \texttt{<type>} is one of
\texttt{cnt} (file), \texttt{dir} (directory), \texttt{rev} (commit), \texttt{rel}
(release), \texttt{snp} (snapshot).

A qualified \textsc{swhid} appends context and fragment qualifiers:

\begin{verbatim}
swh:1:cnt:<hex>;origin=<url>;visit=swh:1:snp:<hex>;
            anchor=swh:1:rev:<hex>;path=/src/file.ml;lines=192-228
\end{verbatim}
