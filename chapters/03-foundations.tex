\chapter{Foundations}
\label{ch:foundations}

To reference code reliably we need the right kind of identifier and the right kind of
archive. This chapter builds the concepts from the ground up: why forges are not archives,
the difference between intrinsic and extrinsic identifiers, the Merkle-DAG data model, and
the \textsc{swhid}.

\takeaway{An \emph{intrinsic} identifier is computed from the content; a DOI is not.}
