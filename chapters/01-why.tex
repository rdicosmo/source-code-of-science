\chapter{Why this note}
\label{ch:why}

Software is the third pillar of open science, alongside publications and data. Yet the
source code behind published results is too often lost, mutated, or impossible to run. This
chapter sets the stakes --- reproducibility and preservation --- and the promise of the rest
of the note: a workflow you can apply starting with your next paper.

\takeaway{Publications, data, \emph{and} software: source code is a first-class research
output.}
